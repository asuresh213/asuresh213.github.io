Graduate Student Seminar at MU

  • Over the course of Fall 2022 - Spring 2024, I co-organized the graduate student seminar at MU with Luis Flores.
  • The seminar meets weekly and welcomes diverse graduate student presentations in mathematics and allied fields.
  • The seminar is currently co-organized by Srishti Singh & Benjamin Ashlock and meets Fridays at 4PM in MSB 110.

Continuum group at GSU

  • In Fall 2018, I founded The continuum group - GSU’s first undergraduate competitive problem solving team.
  • In its very first semester, the team represented debuted GSU in the problem solving stage with representation in
    • The Virginia Tech Regional Math Competition and
    • The Putnam exam
  • The group remains active as a sub-organization of the Math & Stats club and is overseen by Dr. Mark Grinshpon

Organization for the promotion of science (OpSci)

  • During the summer following my high school graduation (2016), I collaborated with undergraduate students from ISI, CMI, IIT Madras, IISc, and IISER to conduct outreach programs at local high schools across Chennai.
  • In these sessions we mentored high school students about pursuing degrees and careers in the sciences, and explored career outcomes and pathways from such a pursuit.
