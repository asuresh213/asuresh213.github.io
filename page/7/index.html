Hi, I am Arun. I am currently a PhD student in mathematics at University of Missouri working under the advisement of Dr. Dan Edidin. My doctoral work is centered around utilizing results from algebraic geometry and representation theory to approach inverse problems arising in data science and computational imaging. I am also interested in applied data science, machine learning and mathematical modeling for epidemiology. Before this, I was fortunate to have been identified and advised by Dr. Florian Enescu at Georgia State University, where I studied commutative algebra.

I established and currently coordinate the Directed Readings Program (DRP) at MU.
