My research (thus far) utilizes results from algebraic geometry and representation theory to say something meaningful about phase-retrieval problems and some associated variations of it. My primary engagement in phase retrieval is with problems emerging from the areas of X-ray crystallography, Multi-reference alignment (MRA), and cryo-electron microscopy (cryo-EM). When I am not busy retrieving phase, I think (and read) more about applied algebraic geometry, topological data analysis and quiver representation theory.
